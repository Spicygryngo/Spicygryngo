## What I Build

I build AI trading infrastructure that connects:

- Quant research
- Strategy backtesting
- Pine-to-Python conversion
- Market regime detection
- Meta-allocation
- Risk governance
- SMA execution routing
- Client dashboards
- Paper → testnet → live deployment workflows

The thesis is simple:

> Static trading algorithms do not hallucinate, but they are regime fragile.  
> AI can adapt, but it must be governed.  
> Tetrad combines deterministic trading systems with an AI governance layer that controls permission, sizing, routing, and risk.

---

## Core Stack

| Layer | System | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Research | Tetrad Lab | Strategy validation, backtesting, candidate promotion, portfolio testing |
| Intelligence | MLTT/RORO | Market regime scoring, risk-on/risk-off detection, strategy permission |
| Execution | Tetrad Tower | Self-custody SMA routing, signal forwarding, client account infrastructure |
| Monitoring | Dashboards | Live signals, fills, PnL, payout tracking, investor reporting |
| Governance | Meta-Allocator | Selects which algorithm trades, how much capital it receives, and when risk is reduced |
